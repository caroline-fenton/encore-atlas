## User Personas

---

### 1. The Nostalgic Fan

**Profile**
- Age: 30–50
- Deep emotional connection to specific artists, scenes, or eras
- Often revisits music tied to personal memories

**Context of Use**
- Evenings, alone or with a partner
- Listening with headphones or casting to a TV
- Revisiting concerts, specific songs, or moments

**Goals**
- Relive concerts and music experiences from the past
- Reconnect with a specific feeling or period of life
- Find performances that feel authentic and emotionally resonant

**Pain Points**
- Hard to find high-quality live versions of songs
- YouTube surfaces unrelated or distracting content
- No easy way to stay within a specific “moment” or vibe

**How Encore Atlas Helps**
- Provides a focused, music-only experience
- Surfaces related performances that match the same energy
- Enables continuous exploration without breaking immersion

---

### 2. The Deep Explorer

**Profile**
- Age: 20–40
- Highly engaged music listener
- Enjoys discovering new artists, scenes, and subgenres

**Context of Use**
- Active listening sessions (often late at night)
- Moves between artists, genres, and eras quickly
- Curious and exploratory mindset

**Goals**
- Go deeper into a genre or scene
- Discover adjacent artists and performances
- Understand connections between artists and styles

**Pain Points**
- Discovery on major platforms is broad but shallow
- Hard to follow a specific musical thread deeply
- Recommendations often feel generic or algorithmic

**How Encore Atlas Helps**
- Surfaces contextually relevant recommendations
- Enables exploration by vibe, era, and scene
- Creates a more intentional discovery experience

---

### 3. The Session Listener

**Profile**
- Age: 20–45
- Treats music as a continuous experience rather than isolated tracks
- Often listens while relaxing, working, or socializing

**Context of Use**
- Passive or semi-passive listening
- Alone, or with friends/family
- Wants the experience to flow without interruption

**Goals**
- Stay in a continuous music session
- Avoid needing to constantly search or decide what’s next
- Maintain a consistent mood or energy

**Pain Points**
- Sessions break after a single video
- Requires constant input to continue
- Switching between apps or searches disrupts flow

**How Encore Atlas Helps**
- Automatically surfaces recommended videos
- Reduces need for manual search
- Supports continuous, immersive sessions

---

### (Future) 4. The Social Curator

**Profile**
- Highly engaged user who wants to share music
- Builds identity through taste and curation

**Goals**
- Share favorite performances with others
- Create collections of meaningful music moments
- Discover what others are watching

**Future Opportunities**
- User profiles
- Shared recommendation lists
- Commentary and interaction around videos
