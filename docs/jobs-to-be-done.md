## Jobs to Be Done

### Core Job

When I’m in the mood to experience live music,  
I want to quickly find and continue watching performances that match a specific energy or memory,  
so I can stay immersed in that feeling without interruption.

---

### Discovery Job

When I’ve just watched a performance I enjoyed,  
I want to be shown more content that feels similar in vibe, era, or scene,  
so I don’t have to think about what to search for next.

---

### Nostalgia Job

When I’m thinking about a concert, artist, or period of my life,  
I want to revisit live performances that capture that moment,  
so I can relive the experience and emotional context around it.

---

### Exploration Job

When I’m interested in a particular artist or genre,  
I want to explore adjacent artists, performances, and scenes,  
so I can go deeper into that musical world.

---

### Passive Engagement Job

When I’m listening alone or with others,  
I want the experience to continue naturally from one performance to the next,  
so I can stay in the session without needing to actively search.

---

### Social / Shared Experience Job (Future)

When I discover a great live performance,  
I want to share or revisit it with others,  
so we can connect over a shared music moment.

---

### Personalization Job (Future)

When I return to the app,  
I want it to reflect my taste and past listening behavior,  
so I can immediately find something I’ll enjoy without starting from scratch.
